Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay and affectionately as "Thalapathy" (Commander), is a prominent Indian actor and a recent entrant into politics. One of India's highest-paid and most commercially successful actors, he is renowned for his action-packed roles, charismatic screen presence, and dancing skills. Early Life and Family: Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vijay comes from a film family, with his father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, a director, and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, a singer. He married Sangeetha Sornalingam in 1999, and they have two children. Film Career: Vijay started his acting career as a child actor and made his debut as a lead in Naalaiya Theerpu (1992). His breakthrough came with Poove Unakkaga (1996), followed by successful films like Kadhalukku Mariyadhai (1997) and Thulladha Manamum Thullum (1999). In the 2000s, he established himself as an action hero with hits such as Ghilli (2004), Pokkiri (2007), and Vettaikaaran (2009). The 2010s and beyond saw continued success with blockbusters including Thuppakki (2012), Kaththi (2014), Mersal (2017), Sarkar (2018), Bigil (2019), Master (2021), and Leo (2023), which became his highest-grossing film. He is known for often portraying multiple roles in his movies. Music and Philanthropy: Vijay is also a playback singer. In 2009, he founded the Vijay Makkal Iyakkam, a social welfare organization involved in community service and relief efforts. Political Career: On February 2, 2024, Vijay launched his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). He has stated that his upcoming film, Jana Nayagan (scheduled for January 2026), will be his last movie as he plans to focus on politics and contest the 2026 Tamil Nadu assembly elections. Awards and Recognition: Considered an influential figure in Indian cinema, Vijay has received multiple awards, including Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. He was awarded an honorary doctorate in 2007 and has been featured on the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list.
